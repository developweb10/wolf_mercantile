About this course

Sponsored by the National Shooting Sports Foundation, this class introduces people who have had no experience with shooting to:

  • The safe and recreational use of firearms
  • The local requirements for owning and purchasing a firearm
  • Shooting sports opportunities for all levels of interest

Participants start off in a classroom setting, move to our simulator to learn about “the feel” of firing a gun then have the opportunity to shoot on the range. Free use of the simulator, free gun and range rental and 20 cartridges are provided.
